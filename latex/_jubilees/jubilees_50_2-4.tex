    \versenum{50:2}
    ወሰንበታተ ፡ ምድርኒ ፡ ነገርኩከ ፡ በደብረ ፡ ሲና ፡ ወዓመታተ ፡
    ኢዮቤሌዎኒ ፡ ውስተ ፡ ሰንበታተ ፡ ዓመታት ፡ ወዓመቶሰ ፡ ኢነገርናከ ፡
    እስከ ፡ አመ ፡ ትበውኡ ፡ ውስተ ፡ ምድር ፡ እንተ ፡ ትእኅዙ ፡
    \versenum{3}
    ወታሰነብት ፡ ምድርኒ ፡ ሰንበታቲሃ ፡ በነቢሮቶሙ ፡ ዲቤሃ ፡ 
    ወዓመቶ ፡ ለኢዮቤል ፡ ያእምሩ ። 
    \versenum{4}
    በእንተዝ ፡ ሠራዕኩ ፡ ለከ ፡

    አምነ ፡ መዋዕሊሁ ፡ ለአዳም ፡ እስከ ፡ ዛቲ ፡ ዕለት ፡ ወሱባዔ ፡
    አሐዱ ፡ ወክልኤ ፡ ዓመታት ፡ ወዓዲ ፡ አርብዓ ፡ ዓመት ፡ 
    ርሑቅ ፡ ለአእምሮ ፡ ትእዛዛተ ፡ እግዚአብሔር ፡ እስከ ፡ አመ ፡ ያዐድው ፡
    ማዕዶተ ፡ ምድረ ፡ ከናአን ፡ ዐዲዎሙ ፡ ዮርዳኖስ ፡ ግጸ ፡ ዐረቢሁ ፡
\end{ethiopictext}
\begin{transliteration}
    \versenum{50:2}
    wa-sanəbatāta mədrə-ni nagarku-ka ba-dabra sinā wa-ʕāmatāta
    % sanbatāta         'sabbath'
    % medre-ni          -ni 'even, that very' Lambdin 51.4
    % nabarku-ka        g pf 1cs √nbr 'to say'
    % sinā              'Sinai'
    % ʕāmatāta          ʕām pl. ʕāmatāt 'year' (Les. 62)
    ʔiyobēlēwo-ni wəsta sanbatāta ʕāmatāt wa-ʕāmato-sa ʔiyyə-nagarnā-ka
    % ʔiyobēlēwo        + 3mpl
    % -sa               -sa 'but' =gk δε see Lambdin 51.4
    % nagarnā           g pf 1cp + 2ms 'to say'
    ʔəska ʔama təbawwəʔu wəsta mədr ʔənta təʔəḫḫezu
    % tebawweʔu          g impf. 2mp √bwʔ Les. 114
    % ʔama              ʔama prep. 'at the time of'
    % teʔeḫḫezu          g impf. 2mp √ʔḫz Les. 14

    \versenum{3}
    wa-tāsanabbət mədrə-ni sanbatāti-hā ba-nabirotomu dibē-hā
    % tāsanabbet        QC impf (quad) √snbt 'to make sabbath'
    % nabirotomu        G inf. + 3mp √nbr 'to live, dwell'
    wa-ʕāmato la-ʔiyobēl yāʔməru
    % ʕāmato            ʕām sn. 'year'
    % yāʔəmmeru         CG subj √ʔmr 'understand, recognize'
    %                   if this was impf e should be a for I-guttural
    \versenum{4}
    ba-ʔenta-ze śarāʕku laka
    % sarāʕku           g pf 1cs √srʕ 'establish'
    subāʕē ʕematāt wa-ʕiyobēlēwotāta ʔerbʕ wa-tasʕatu ʔiyobēlēwotāt
    the weeks of years and the Jubilees: 49 Jubilees
    % subāʕē            'week'
    ʔemmena mawāʕlihu la-ʔadām ʔeska zāti ʕelat wa-subāʕē
    from the time of Adam to this day. And one week
    % 
    ʔeḥadu wa-kelʔē ʕāmatāt waʕādi ʔerbʕā ʕemat
    and two years
    % 
    reḥuq laʔaʔmro teʔezāzāta ʔegzi-ʔabḥēr ʔeska ʔama yaʕadwu
    % 
    māʕdota medra kanāʔan ʕadiwomu yordānos geṣṣ ʕarabihu

\end{transliteration}

\begin{translation}
    \versenum{50:2}
    I told you [about] the sabbaths of the land [while] on Mt. Sinai and the years of
    Jubilees in the sabbaths of the years. But we did not tell you its year
    until the time that you enter the land, which you will possess.
    % VanderKam: On Mt. Sinai I told you about the sabbaths of the land and the years of
    % jubilees in the sabbaths of the years, but its year we have not told you 
    % until the time when you enter the land which you will possess.
    \versenum{3}
    And the land will observe its sabbath when they dwell upon it
    that they may know the year of Jubilee.
    % the land will observe its sabbaths when they live on it,
    % and they are to know the year of the jubilee.
    \versenum{4}
    Because of this I have established for you
    % For this reason I have arranged for you the weeks of years and the jubilees
    % ---49 jubilees from the time of Adam until today, 
    % and one week and two years. I is still 40 years off 
    % (for learning the Lord's commandments) until the time when he leads (them)
    % across to the land of Canaan, after they have crossed the Jordan to the west of it.

    \emph{**VanderKam's translation (in the interest of time). I will provide my own text and translation in subsequent drafts}
\end{translation}
